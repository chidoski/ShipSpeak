## Critical Framework Integration

### PM Career Transition Patterns (MANDATORY)
The foundation must support all PM career progression paths:

#### Executive PM Maintenance & Mastery Foundation
- **Quarterly Board Excellence**: Premium design quality appropriate for investor review and board presentation materials
- **Industry Speaking Mastery**: Professional visual standards suitable for conference presentations and thought leadership positioning
- **Crisis Communication**: Design systems supporting rapid response interfaces and stakeholder confidence maintenance
- **Organizational Leadership**: UI patterns optimized for all-hands presentations and strategic vision cascading

### Multi-Dimensional PM Competency Integration
Foundation must support 5-point radar chart visualization:

#### 1. Product Sense & Strategic Thinking (20-95% scale)
- **Visual Design**: Color coding and iconography for user problem identification, solution evaluation, market context awareness
- **Framework Support**: UI patterns supporting RICE, ICE, Jobs-to-be-Done methodology presentation

#### 2. Communication & Executive Presence (15-90% scale)
- **Executive Typography**: Professional font systems appropriate for C-suite review and board presentation
- **Answer-First Structure**: Layout patterns supporting conclusion-driven communication for executive audiences

#### 3. Stakeholder Management & Cross-Functional Leadership (25-85% scale)
- **Multi-Audience Design**: Component systems adapting to engineer, designer, executive, customer contexts
- **Conflict Resolution**: UI patterns supporting difficult conversation facilitation and consensus building

#### 4. Technical Translation & Data Fluency (30-80% scale)
- **Complexity Simplification**: Visual hierarchy supporting technical concept translation for business audiences
- **Data Visualization**: Chart and graph systems optimized for analytics presentation and metrics communication

#### 5. Business Impact & Organizational Leadership (10-95% scale)
- **ROI Visualization**: Design systems connecting product decisions to business outcomes and revenue impact
- **Resource Allocation**: UI patterns supporting headcount, budget, timeline decision communication
