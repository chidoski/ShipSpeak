## Critical Progress Tracking Architecture

### Core Tracking Categories (MANDATORY)
The progress tracking system must capture complete user learning journey:

#### Meeting Intelligence Progress
- **Processing Progress**: Real-time meeting analysis progress tracking
- **Analysis Completion**: Meeting analysis completion rates and timelines
- **Insight Generation**: AI insight quality and relevance tracking
- **Usage Patterns**: Meeting upload frequency and patterns
- **Processing Success**: Analysis success rates and failure tracking

#### Practice Session Tracking
- **Session Completion**: Practice session completion rates
- **Skill Development**: Individual skill area progress measurement
- **Exercise Performance**: Exercise-level performance tracking
- **Time Investment**: Practice time tracking and optimization
- **Improvement Velocity**: Rate of skill development measurement

#### Learning Outcome Metrics
- **Competency Growth**: 5-dimensional PM competency radar tracking
- **Strength Identification**: Area of excellence identification and reinforcement
- **Gap Closure**: Skill gap reduction measurement
- **Performance Consistency**: Consistency of performance across sessions
- **Real-world Application**: Meeting performance improvement correlation

### Privacy-Safe Analytics Integration
Foundation must provide comprehensive analytics while maintaining user privacy:

#### Privacy Standards
- **ID-Only Tracking**: No personally identifiable information storage
- **Aggregated Metrics**: Individual data aggregated for insights
- **Consent Management**: User control over analytics data sharing
- **Data Minimization**: Collect only necessary data for insights
- **Retention Control**: User-controlled data retention periods

#### Usage Analytics (Privacy-Safe)
- **Feature Usage**: Platform feature utilization patterns
- **Engagement Metrics**: User engagement and retention metrics
- **Performance Trends**: System performance and optimization metrics
- **Error Tracking**: System error patterns and resolution tracking
- **Success Metrics**: Platform effectiveness measurement
